Join Our Loyalty Program!

Welcome to our loyalty program! Sign up today and start enjoying a range of benefits designed to make your shopping experience even better. Whether you’re a frequent shopper or just looking to save on your grocery bills, we have a loyalty scheme that suits your needs.

Benefits:

  • • Earn 1 point for every Rs.100 spent.
  • • Receive a Rs.500 voucher for every 500 points.
  • • Access to exclusive member-only discounts.

The Silver Loyalty Scheme is perfect for occasional shoppers who want to start earning rewards on every purchase.

Benefits:

  • • Earn 1.5 points for every Rs.100 spent.
  • • Receive a Rs.1000 voucher for every 500 points.
  • • Priority access to sales events.
  • • Monthly discount coupons.

The Gold Loyalty Scheme is ideal for regular shoppers looking for extra savings and exclusive benefits.

Benefits:

  • • Earn 2 points for every Rs.100 spent.
  • • Receive a Rs.2500 voucher for every 500 points.
  • • Free home delivery for orders over $50
  • • Early access to new products.
  • • Exclusive invites to VIP events.

The Platinum Loyalty Scheme is designed for our most loyal customers who enjoy a premium shopping experience with maximum rewards.
