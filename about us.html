Our locations

Find our premium dairy products at various locations across the city. Visit our stores to experience the freshest dairy products close to you.

Colombo Central Branch

Located in the heart of Colombo, our Central Branch offers a wide range of products from fresh produce to household essentials. With spacious aisles and a friendly staff, shopping here is a pleasant experience. We also offer special services such as home delivery and online ordering to make your life easier.

Kandy Hills Branch

Nestled in the scenic hills of Kandy, our branch provides a serene shopping environment. Enjoy our extensive range of organic and locally sourced products. Our dedicated team is always ready to assist you with personalized service. Additionally, we offer catering services for all your event needs.

Galle Fort Branch

Situated in the historic Galle Fort, this branch combines heritage charm with modern convenience. Discover unique local products and international brands in our well-stocked aisles. Our Galle Fort branch also provides party planning services to help you create memorable celebrations.
